How it works

Hardware hub works in four simple steps

1-SEARCH:

All you have to do is enter your location and the materials you need and Hardware Hub will provide you a list of close and certified hardware stores to choose from.

2-CONTACT STORE:

Once you have chosen a hardware store, Hardware Hub provides for you to contact the store that has impressed you. To contact your store, you just need to create account.

3-BROWSE:

Hardware Hub avails a list of certified hardware stores closest to you at all times. In addition, you get the availabe stock and the respective prices of hardware for the gamut of hardware stores available.

4-MEET DEADLINES:

When you have agreed with your store, You can then focus on your other activities that you would have otherwise put on hold to go looking for the hardware store.
